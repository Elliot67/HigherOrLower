# Higher Or Lower I Know

## What is higher Or Lower I Know ?
**Higher Or Lower I Know** is a non official extension for the game [HigherOrLower](www.higherlowergame.com). It enable you to beat your highscore without any effort by saving all the data given by the game.

## How do I use the web extension ?
You simply have to go to 'The Higher Lower Game' website, start a game and click once on the extension icon. The extension will now play for you and save all the data by restarting games one after the others until you leave the website. All the data will be saved in a file you can consult by going on www.higherlowergame.com/settings and clicking on the extension icon.

## I don't want to wait for my highscore, how do I do ?
No problem, I already saved a huge part of the data, you just need to [copy the data](www.pastebin.com/z7Z2MhfM) and paste it in the area at [higherlowergame.com/settings](www.higherlowergame.com/settings) once you have clicked the extension icon.

## Note:
- The data given is not totaly complete, some words are missing.
- The data is based on global monthly searches in 2017. This means it can be obsolete from 2019.
- Once you clicked on the extension it will be still active until you close the tab. Make sur to close it after using it and not to open it twice.
